Ronay Poole

Bio

Ronay is a recent graduate of the Guildford School of Acting. Originally from The Midlands, Ronay is an actor, voice artist and singer. Ronay has just finished filming the short 'I Need To Tell You Something' with Firewood Pictures and most recently performed at The Royal Court Theatre in Douglas Hodge’s rehearsed reading of Harold Pinter's 'Mountain Language'.
