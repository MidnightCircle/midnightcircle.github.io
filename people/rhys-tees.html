Rhys Tees

Bio

Rhys trained at Millennium Performing Arts where he left with a diploma in acting and musical theatre. Armed with a love of comedy and improvisation, Rhys has found himself at home within the two mediums over the past few years performing in immersive groups such as ‘Easel Peasel’ (Glastonbury, Nozstock festival) and appearing in several runs of the Guinness world record holding sketch comedy show ‘News Revue’ (Canal cafe theatre) Rhys is also heavily featured in Radio Stakanovs football mockumentary podcast series ‘The Offensive’ playing football club owner Hutch Lewis as well as many other additional voices.

Other credits include: The Importance of being...Earnest? (The Pleasance), The Twelve Irish Tenors (American Tour) Johnny Shot (Canal Cafe), Lottery (Pleasance), We Own Everything (The Space), The Frontier Trilogy (Hen and Chickens), The Gravediggers (Old Red Lion), Swaps (The Bunker), RENT (Broadway theatre).
