Jack Masefield

Bio

Jack is an actor and fight director who has been performing and working in the industry for four years. He trained at Derby university gaining a BA(hons) in Theatre as well as then training in martial arts and HEMA. Jack has also completed his qualifications in stunt and fight performing for screen and stage with ECPSC and Take 3 in unarmed combat, dagger, arming sword, sword and shield, long sword, saber, rapier and dagger and quarter staff. Jack has been been involved with several short and independent films as a stunt performer and his previous theatre credits include a production of "Pronoun", "Fugee", "Two" by Jim Cartwright and Shakespeare’s "Macbeth".
