Zoe Dawkins

Bio

Trained at Bath Spa University, Zoë is a varied actor who favours live events and immersive theatre. In this style she has worked for household names such as Tulley's Farm, Thorpe Park and The London Bridge Tombs. As a proudly dedicated bookworm, she's delighted to be a part of Immersive Dracula; bringing Bram Stoker's epic tale to life.
