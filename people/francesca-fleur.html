Francesca Fleur

Bio

Francesca recently graduated from Warwick university where she received a BA honours in English and Theatre studies. Francesca’s professional credits include acting at the London Dungeons, performing as John in ‘what are they like’ at the lyric Theatre in Hammersmith, as well as various tv commercials and feature films airing in 2020. Francesca’s niche in the acting industry is immersive theatre as well as improvisation, she has performed one of her own self published murder mysteries to a group inmates at Hewell prison.
