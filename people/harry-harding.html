Harry Harding

Bio

Harry attended Hampstead Fine Arts College before training at Cambridge School of Art & Drama where he achieved a BA in Drama (Hons). He is the founder and artistic director of Room One Theatre Company. He is also a playwright, having written three one-act plays.

This is Harry’s third production of ‘Dracula’, having previously played both the title character and Van Helsing. Previous immersive experience includes a six month contract at The London Dungeon.
