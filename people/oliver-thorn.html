Oliver Thorn

Bio

A professional actor and YouTuber, in 2019 Oliver made headlines by performing the complete works of Shakespeare, streaming live over the Internet 24/7 for more than 100 hours, raising $130,000 for Samaritans.

He is also the creator of Philosophy Tube, a webshow with an audience of over half a million. Trained at East 15, his other credits include Claudio in Much Ado About Nothing (UK Tour), Thursday (Southwark Playhouse), HAMM (RADA Festival), Still (short film) and commercials for Stylist Magazine, Brita, and Astralis.
